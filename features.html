Product Overview:

The LadderTOP device provides a secure point at where the ladder meets the upper structure. The device offers a means of "Tie Off Point" while the design offers inherent protection from "ladder slide". "Ladder slide" occurs particularly when stepping off or onto the ladder while ascending/descending. Ladder slide is one of the leading causes of injury resulting from extension ladder usage. High wind conditions can also lead to ladder slide.

This safety device will greatly reduce the risk for falls and the serious injuries and possible death associated with falls from elevated heights. This will help reduce workers' compensation and insurance claims, and has the potential of becoming a "standard building requirement".

Fundamental Design

  • The LadderTOP device mounts to a secure structure and utilizes two side plates that surround the ladder to prevent the ladder from sliding in either direction. This eliminates the risk from falling due to ladder slippage.
  • The device also incorporates holes in the side plates to be utilized for a tie off point to further secure the ladder and place a downward pressure on the ladder to reduce base movement.

Cosmetic Design

  • The device is cosmetically designed to blend into the surrounding surfaces for permanently mounted applications, ready to use on the initial ladder access and final descent.
  • Temporary installation provides safety protection during work periods for critical accents and descents.
  • The device is made from aluminum to prevent rusting and unsightly discoloration.
  • LadderTOP is available in a non-painted aluminum version to blend in with aluminum capping and other metal structures. It is also available in two standard colors (white and brown) to match standard gutter colors. Non-standard colors are also available upon special order.
